About Me

Leader, thinker, public speaker and collaborator focused on shaping a thriving future for all through international cooperation and leadership.

My Journey

As someone who overcame poverty through hard work and a deep commitment to leadership as a value, I have developed a unique perspective on global challenges and opportunities. My journey from humble beginnings to becoming a voice in international affairs has shaped my approach to collaboration, advocacy, and systems change.

I believe that the most pressing issues of our time—from climate change to international cooperation, from human rights to the impacts of Artifical Intelligence —require not just technical solutions but fundamentally new ways of thinking about our shared future. Drawing on my experiences across sectors, connections and countries, I work to bridge divides and create innovative pathways forward.

With a background spanning activism, public relations, campaign strategy, and advocacy, I bring both analytical rigor and practical experience to complex global challenges. My work is driven by a persistent optimism that better futures are possible when we collaborate across boundaries and center diverse perspectives, particularly from the frontline communities.
